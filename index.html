Champaran Mutton Curry

It is historically cooked in earthen pots or matka on dum model. In different phrases, the mutton is positioned contained in the matka, which is then lined from high and sealed by wheat dough to forestall the steam from going out. The mutton, intermixed with spices, is then cooked on low warmth. The steam inside make the mutton strong and decadent!

Hydrebadi Chicken Biryani

Hyderabadi biryani is originated in Hyderabad (a city of India) in the kitchen of Nizam of Hyderabad. It is a blend of two different cuisines Mughlai and Irani. Chicken is the main ingredient for making Hyderabadi chicken dum biryani. It is made with basmati rice, chicken & flavored with Indian spices.
