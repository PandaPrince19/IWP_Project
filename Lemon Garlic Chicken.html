Lemon Garlic Chicken

Prep: 15mins
Cook: 25 mins
Yields: 4 Servings
For Home Chefs

01.

Cut the chicken breasts in half horizontally to make them thinner. This helps them cook faster. Sprinkle each with salt and pepper to taste then dredge each in flour. Melt the butter in a large skillet and brown the chicken breasts. About 3 minutes on each side.

02.

While the chicken is browning prepare your remaining ingredients. This is important because the recipe goes fast and you donâ€™t want to burn the garlic while measuring the other ingredients.

03.

Remove the chicken and set aside. Add the garlic to the pan and stir constantly for 30 seconds. Add remaining ingredients stirring well to combine.

04.

Add chicken back to the pan and turn the chicken to coat in the sauce.
