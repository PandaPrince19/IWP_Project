Champaran Mutton Curry

Prep: 30mins
Cook: 1hr 15 mins
Yields: 4 Servings
For Beginners

01.

In a pan take two tbsp coriander seeds, one tbsp cumin, pepper corns, 5-8 cloves and 5-6 dried red chilli and some salt. Slightly roast them on a low flame and grind into fine powder.

02.

In a bowl add 500 gm mutton, add some salt and 500 gm onions, 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste and half of the ground mutton masala.

03.

In a pressure cooker, add 1/2 cup mustard oil, and let it smoke

04.

Add three red chillies, cumin, bay leaf , green chillies and stir for a while

05.

Add the mutton pieces mixed in masala, some garlic pods and cook in low flame for 45 mins while stirring in between.

06.

Once the mutton is cooked well , add rest of the masala 2tbsp ghee, green chilli and cook for another 2 mins

07.

Add one cup water and Pressure cook the mutton for one whistle finally and its done.
